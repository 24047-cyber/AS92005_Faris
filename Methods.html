Methods

How our water is managed

  1. 1. Screening: Filters large debris through metal screens.
  2. 2. Coagulation: Clumps contaminants using aluminium sulphate.
  3. 3. Clarification (Sedimentation): Settles and removes heavy particles.
  4. 4. Filtration: Traps remaining particles using sand.
  5. 5. Disinfection: Kills germs by adding chlorine.
  6. 6. Fluoridation: Adds fluoride to the supply.
  7. 7. pH Balancing: Normalises pH in the final stage.
