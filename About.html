What you can do about your water quality

While Auckland (NZ) has a relatively clean and safe water supply, it depends on factors such as your household's pipes and the area's tanks.

If you see any changes in water consistency, taste, or colour, you should alert authorities, and they can send someone to check your water's quality to ensure their systems are working correctly, and to resolve the issue. You can buy extra filters (such as reverse osmosis or whole-house filters) to remove further contaminants and chemicals.
