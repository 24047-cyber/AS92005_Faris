What you can do and why you should do it

While Auckland has a relatively clean and safe water supply, it depends on factors such as your household's pipes and the area's tanks.
If you see any changes in water consistency, taste, or colour, you should alert authorities, and they can send someone over to check your water's quality to ensure their systems are working correctly.
You can buy extra filters (such as reverse osmosis filters) to remove further contaminants and chemicals.
